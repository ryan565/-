About Rose Angel

Art that breathes life into your space

Hi! I'm Dani — the artist behind Rose Angel Art. Originally from Colombia 🇨🇴 and now proudly calling Perth, Western Australia home, I bring the vibrant colours and joyful energy of my roots to every wall I paint.

I believe businesses deserve more than bare walls. A hand-painted mural tells your customers who you are before a single word is spoken. My style blends bold tropical botanicals, expressive brushwork, and a warmth that only comes from art made entirely by hand.

Whether you're after a statement feature wall in your café, a branded mural for your office, or something totally bespoke — let's make it beautiful together.

How it works

From blank wall to masterpiece

💬

1. Free Consultation

We chat about your vision, the space, your brand, and your budget. No obligation, just ideas.

✏️

2. Design & Concept

I create a full design mockup showing exactly how the mural will look on your wall. You give feedback until it's perfect.

🎨

3. Paint Day(s)

I arrive with everything needed and get to work. Most business murals are completed in 1–3 days with minimal disruption.

✨

4. Reveal & Touch-ups

Stand back and admire your wall. I do any final touches and you get photos to share with the world.
